What are we about

Agala klub 12 is a non-political and non-profit social club formed on the ethic of communal unity. Formed in 1985 by a group of friends; Agala Klub 12 aims to give back to the community that was instrumental to their development. Since its inception the organization has been responsible for a myriad of charity, community development and community outreach projects in the Ago-Taylor Area of Ibadan. Agala Klub 12 is now registered with Corporate Affairs Commission, Nigeria.

What we do

Agala Klub 12 was founded on the ethics of philanthropy, our purpose is:

- To give back to the community that raised through charitable projects, in form of scholarships and lending a helping hand to the needy

- To continue to sensitize members on ways of improving their lives through quality management of resources, entrepreneurship creativity.

- Contribute to the alleviation of poverty in the community
